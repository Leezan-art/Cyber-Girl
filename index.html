About Us

Cyber-Girls is an interactive website that engages young girls in an environment that lets them use their cybersecurity skills to save CyberWorld. We are a group of girls who believe that cybersecurity is a field clouded by misguided stereotypes and aim to clear them for girls at younger ages.
